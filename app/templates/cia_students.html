What is CIA?

Coimbatore Industrial Association (CIA) is a premier network of 200+ leading industrial and manufacturing companies in Coimbatore. We are committed to bridging the gap between educational institutions and the industry by providing students with:

  • Real-world Industrial Experience: Work directly with established manufacturing companies
  • Comprehensive Training Programs: Learn cutting-edge technologies and industry practices
  • Entrepreneurship Support: Mentorship to launch your own venture
  • Industry Connections: Network with leading professionals and company leaders
  • Career Opportunities: Direct placement opportunities in partner companies
